About PapersortingMood

Paper sorting mood is a collection of different genre of EDM songs that we usually listen while working.

I hate that my colleagues always have to look for a certain song on Youtube for every f*cking time.I personally hate Youtube, so many ads.

That's why I create this site using SoundClound API. Single click. Non-stop music. No ads anymore

PapersortingMood includes Progressive House, Future House, Trap, Dubstep, Tropical House, Ambient and so on. I divided those genres into 2 categories: Partymood and Chillmood.
